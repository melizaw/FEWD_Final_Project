"I have assisted and have been the ghost baker on various projects including Sunset Magazine, Target and Driscoll’s.”

Amy earned a degree in Pastry and Baking at the California Culinary Academy and attended the Culinary Institute of America in Sonoma. After graduating, she worked at Postrio under Susan Brinkley and then at Patisserie Lambert under pastry chef, Sarah Lambert; where she expanded her repertoire of French pastries. Later, Amy founded AmyCakes, in 1998 and went onto become Executive Pastry Chef at Paula LeDuc Fine Catering in San Francisco. In 2005, Amy advised Allison Barakat of Bakesale Betty, and worked directly with Williams-Sonoma, launching a line of products and cookbooks. Amy is currently a consultant for Meyer Corporation on the Cake Boss Bakeware line and continues to do recipe development for www.potsandpans.com and Cake Boss Baking. and assist in styling food for various clients, including Sunset Magazine, Target, Kikkoman, Driscoll’s, and more.
